DELIVERY POLICY

On-demand Orders, Advance Orders and Notifications

We accept on-demand orders with just a 30 - 45 minutes lead time. We also accept advance orders up to [1 month] prior to your delivery date. Please select your preferred delivery date and time upon check-out. You will receive a text confirmation after placing your order. We may call or text you directly, if necessary. You will also be notified via text if your order has been dispatched for delivery.

Area of Coverage and Fees

Delivery fees will be calculated upon check-out, before proceeding to payment. Delivery fees may range from P100 to P350 depending on the distance of your location from the cafe. Please note that distance may affect the quality of the food due to longer delivery time. If your location is not covered by our delivery area, please contact us through our phone number found in the “Contact Us” page to see if we can accommodate your area.

Possibility of Delays

The delivery time slot for the next available hour does not take into account the distance of your delivery address. When selecting time slots, please note that peak hours, rainy weather, and holidays may affect delivery time due to volume of orders and heavier road traffic. We appreciate your understanding.

Store / Curbside Pick-Up Option

You may also choose to select the “Store Pick-Up” option and arrange to pick-up personally. If you need our staff to deliver your food to your car by the driveway, please indicate “Curbside Pick-Up” along with your vehicle type, color, and plate number in the “Special Request” portion. For curbside pick-up, please call us thru our phone number to inform us when you have arrived. There will be no added fees for the Store Pick-Up option and the Curbside Pick-Up Service.

Payment

Payment may be made online via BDO Bank Transfer and GCash. In the unlikely case you are not able to make an online payment, you may choose the “Offline Bank Transfer/Cash on Delivery (COD)” option. In this case, your order is not confirmed until our staff directly contacts you and accepts your order. Please note that COD may entail additional delivery fees due to the double trip for cash handling and may not always be approved for farther locations and huge amounts.

Thank you for your patience and understanding as we work hard to fulfill each order safely and with care.
