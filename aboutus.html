About Us

Welcome to Fur the Coffee !

People near Tanauan need not just a coffee, pastries, or pastas, but also a place where we can share bonds comfortably, or have a group discussion, study, work or read.

With the help of Lihim Café, we collaborated with them to bring this cafe to life, that is the reason why Fur the Coffee is established. A cafe that is determined to become a daily necessity for coffee lovers. A perfect spot to unwind, relax, and release all your stress all in one.

Our Business

Fur the Coffee is our new pet-friendly cafe in partnership with Lihim Cafe.

It offers a wide variety of affordable and delicious coffee and great tasting food prepared to satisfy customers of all ages and from all walks of life. With every spice, sauce, and spoonful of our dishes, Fur the Coffee shares with you the comfort of our coffee and food that you will forever rave and crave.

Food quality, service, price-value relationship, store location and ambiance, and efficient operations will be the critical elements of the business' success.
